South Side Hackerspace

We are a group of hackers on the south side of chicago (most of us live around bridgeport/pilsen), who are putting together a social and collaborative space for ourselves and the community.

We want hackers, tinkeres, hobbyists, crafters, artists and doers to take part in this spectacular and fantastical social experiment.

How can I get involved?

If you want timely but not-annoying updates on our progress, just jam your email address into that form below and we'll be in touch.
